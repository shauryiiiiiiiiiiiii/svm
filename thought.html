" You have dealt with so much, and done the best that you can, take a moment now to appreciate how strong you are. - Karen Salmansohn "



MEANING


Published: Today at 12:00 AM | By: Shaurya

This thought of the day by Karen Salmansohn tells us that we should always get time to appreciate what we have done so far even if it is not a big thing in the eyes of others. We have done our best and passed many difficulties in doing all this. Therefore, whatever others may say we must appreciate ourselves for being so strong.
